## Description  (max 4000)
Unwind is a calm, private space to breathe, reflect, and remember to slow down.

No accounts. No ads. No tracking. Everything you write and every breath you take stays on your device.

BREATHE
A guided breathing timer built around a soft, expanding circle. Choose a length, tap to begin, and follow the gentle rhythm. On a physical device, each breath is matched by a quiet haptic "wind chime" — low wooden taps that swell as you inhale and settle as you exhale — so you can close your eyes and feel the pace instead of watching it.

JOURNAL
Capture what's on your mind in a clean, distraction-free editor. Title and entry flow together as one page. Every entry is saved privately on your device.

REMINDERS
Set gentle nudges to unwind. Pick a time and the days of the week, and Unwind will remind you with a local notification — no reminder data ever leaves your phone.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Unwind collects nothing. There is no analytics, no network connection, and no third parties. Your journal and reminders live only in the app, on your device. Delete the app and it's all gone.

Take a minute. Breathe. Unwind.
